ℹ️ How It Works

  1. Upload PDF — Select the exam paper PDF file
  2. Enter Details — Provide college ID, subject code, exam date/time, and principal email
  3. AES-256 Encryption — PDF content is encrypted with military-grade AES-256
  4. RSA-4096 Encryption — AES key is encrypted with RSA-4096 for the principal
  5. Blockchain Storage — Encrypted data stored immutably on Ethereum blockchain
  6. Email Delivery — Paper details (ID, Subject, Timestamp) sent to principal
  7. Time-Lock — Principal can only decrypt after exam date/time is reached
